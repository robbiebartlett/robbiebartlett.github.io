My Work

About

My career has been a journey of teaching, human interaction and digital design. I enjoy taking an empathetic approach for creating interactive design experiences for everyone.

I take a different perspective on the digital world having overcome health challenges. This lead to a unique approach to meeting user needs through how people think, feel and react.

My current role as a designer is a combination of advising and developing UX research, Accessible design and Front-end development.

Outside of design, I'm a train buff, and enjoy cycling and hiking our great outdoors.
