About

  • Organisations I've worked with: Seequent, BusinessNZ, New Zealand Customs Service, Ministry of Social Development, iPayroll/CloudPayroll
  • Industry areas: 3d Software, Business advocacy, Border protection, Social development, accessibility

I'm a UX/UI designer working in Wellington, New Zealand. By combining trend awareness with an empathetic, user centred approach, I seek to understand how people think and feel, and translate those insights into meaningful experiences..

I began in multimedia with a focus on 3D animation, before moving into UX theory and design, with an emphasis on user research and accessible design.

As an experience designer, I work across UX, UI, visual design, animation, accessibility, and front-end development to deliver cohesive, inclusive experiences.
