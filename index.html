Hi, I'm Rob

Researcher, Designer and Developer driven by inclusive design. Currently, I'm based in Wellington, designing user experiences for iPayroll Ltd.

About

I enjoy taking an empathetic approach for creating interactive user experiences for everyone.

With a different perspective on the digital world having overcome health challenges I take an inclusive design approach, meeting user needs through how all people think, feel and react.

As a User experience(UX) designer my work is a combination of UX/UI, Visual design, Animation and Front-end development.

New Zealand Customs Service

Visual Design Iconography

To simplify the usability of the NZ Customs Frontline Mobility App, (nicknamed ‘COLIN’ as a shortening of ‘Customs Online’) iconography was required to meet the project theme.

All icons were drawn on a pixel-based grid of 32px x 32px and scaled down linearly to ensure a unified approach.

The icons were tested for recognition as well as A|B split testing to assist in developing the final set.
