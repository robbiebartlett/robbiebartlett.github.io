About

My career has been a journey of teaching, human interaction and digital design. I enjoy taking an empathetic approach for creating interactive user experiences for everyone.

I take a different perspective on the digital world having overcome health challenges. This lead to an inclusive approach to meeting user needs through how all people think, feel and react.

My current role as a Senior designer is a combination of advising and developing UX research, UI design and Front-end development.

Outside of design, I'm a train buff, and enjoy cycling and hiking our great outdoors.
