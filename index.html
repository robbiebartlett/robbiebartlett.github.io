Hello, I'm Rob

A Researcher, Designer and Developer based in Wellington, New Zealand - Driven by inclusive design and improving digital experiences.

About

My career has been a journey of teaching, human interaction and user centered design. I enjoy taking an empathetic approach for creating interactive user experiences for everyone.

I take a different perspective on the digital world having overcome health challenges. This lead to an inclusive design approach, meeting user needs through how all people think, feel and react.

As a User experience(UX) designer my work is a combination of UX/UI, Visual design and Front-end development.

Outside of design, I'm a train buff, and enjoy cycling and hiking our great outdoors.
