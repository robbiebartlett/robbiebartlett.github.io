About

By day I currently work as a Designer advising, visuallizing and developing engaging online experiences. My work is a fusion of User Experience, Visual Design, Front End Development and Web Production, with the occasional return to animation.

I initially started out teaching and enjoy taking an empathetic approach of human interaction, technology and design into interactive visual experiences. When I'm not designing, coding or animating stuff, I'll be hanging out at home, on the trains or probably cycling somewhere.

What is a UX Unicorn

Remember back in the ‘90’s when the internet was in its infancy and a Webmaster would maintain a website? When sites were less visually complex before the need for a Web Designer and Developer?

Technology exploded and the work became more complex dividing the roles apart to client side involving User Experience and Front-end through to the server side of Back-end development and database administration.

Today the complexity of devices has now split these roles even further with Front-end developers having an identity crisis!  On one side of the fence is an army of developers whose responsibilities revolve around javascript engineering and frameworks such as Vue, React and Angular. Whilst on the other side developers are focussed on HTML, CSS, design patterns and User Accessibility.

As a Unicorn I haven’t quite started sprouting hooves or a horn just yet, but particularly shine in environments where the visual experience is more complex in serving the clients needs. As important is combining wider teams and drawing on all strengths from User Experience, Visual Design and Front-end Development.
