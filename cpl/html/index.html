Highlighted Features

Our feature-rich payroll solution future-proofs your payroll process. To ensure we deliver reliable, simple, and proven features, we listen to our customers’ feedback, keep on top of payroll legislation, and test our software robustly.

  • Human Resources

    With our cloud-based payroll solution you can securely and conveniently upload, store and access employee HR documents.

  • Timelogs

    Paper timesheets can be a thing of the past. With Timelogs, employees can record their hours worked via their Employee Kiosk.

  • Leave Management

    Employees can apply for leave via their Employee Kiosk, removing any prior manual process that may have been in place.

  • User Roles

    Multiple levels of secure user access, according to each employee's role in the payroll process, can be set up.

  • Reporting Suite

    Our comprehensive report suite provides you with 50+ reports including financial, HR, leave, tax, history, and payroll reports.

  • General Ledger postings

    You can download the general ledger accounts in iPayroll, then upload to your chosen accounting system.
