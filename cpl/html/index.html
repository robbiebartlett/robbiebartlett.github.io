The payroll solution for successful businesses

Our easy-to-use, feature-rich payroll solution makes it simple for you or your clients to run each pay and manage leave, PAYG, superannuation payments and employee records. Suitable for all sizes of businesses to 1000 employees, in any industry.

  • Customer Support and Training

    Our exceptional support team are always available via phone or email to answer your payroll questions during business hours. We pride ourselves on our quick responses. Comprehensive training is provided as part of the set-up.

  • Reporting Suite

    Our comprehensive reporting suite includes 50+ reports including financial, HR, leave and payroll tax reports. Wages, superannuation, and allowances can be split over multiple cost centres through to general ledger.

  • Employee Kiosk

    Available via mobile phone and via web. Employees can see payslips, income statements, apply for leave, project leave balance for a future date, view leave calendar and enter hours worked via timelogs, if required.

  • User Roles

    Multiple levels of secure user access are available according to their role. For instance, data entry no salaries, enquiry only (cannot make changes), leave approver who can only see their team and no personal information.

  • Automated superannuation payments

    Register with our clearing house, Superchoice. Superannuation payments are automatically made each month, no action required.
