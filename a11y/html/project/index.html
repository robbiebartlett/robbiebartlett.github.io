Product management

Product managers play a vital role in embedding accessibility requirements throughout each project.

Does each person within your team know their own specific responsibility? Are you keeping track of what needs to be done to deliver accessible products and services?

Accessibility means making products work for all people. To be truly accessible, test all products with real people (including disabled people).

Follow the steps on each page. They aren't fully comprehensive to meet all facets of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). But they do take key elements you need to know into account.

Be aware of accountability to the Legal and policy requirements for accessible websites.

Take a short course - most are free. The 4 to 6 week options are a good starting point for Product managers.

Get in touch with the Accessibility Team, it's a great starting place to get advice and support.
